What is Alzheimer's?


Azlheimer's is a type of dementia that causes problems with memory, think and behavior. Dementia is the term used for memory loss and other abilities that can seriously impact your daily life. No treatments stop or reverse its progression. People with Azlheimr's will live an average of eight years but it can go from four to 20 years depending on age and health conditions.

Help is There!


If you or a loved one has been diagnosed with Alzheimer's or related dementia, dont be scared help is there. The Alzheimer's Association is the trusted resource for reliable information, education, and support for everyone affected by the disease.
